Merge Armor

C1: Heavy Armor
C2: Same outfit, no armor

Method
1. Equip Israel parachute on C1 outfit.
2. Lag C1 with netcut. (Bandwith 5kbps, incoming 7500)
3. Login to gta on C2 and start an invite only session.
4. Equip C2 outfit then re-equip your Israel parachute bag, Wait for the yellow circle.
5. Go to storymode then fully close GTA.
6. Remove your parachute on C1 then unlag console with netcut.
7. Wait for a "Save Successful" message. (Buy ammo / take easy way out if needed)
8. Quit to storymode.
9. Start an invite only session.
10. Start It's a G Thing (https://socialclub.rockstargames.com/job/gtav/zAszPq2OhUGOzIwVXH9PaA)
11. Set clothing to Player Owned Clothing.
12. Invite someone and start the job.
13. If your outfit with Crew Armor is in slot 1, Press Right D-Pad then Left D-Pad.
13_1. If your outfit with Crew Armor is in slot 20, Press Left D-Pad then Right D-Pad.
14. Ready up, an wait for the job to start.
15. Save outfit in apartment. (Refer to SaveInJob.txt)
16. Quit job using phone.
17. After you load into online, You'll have the outfit you started the job with.
18. Equip the outfit you saved in the job.
19. You now have colored armor on your outfit.
