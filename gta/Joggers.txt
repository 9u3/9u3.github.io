Joggers

Method:
1. Equip C1 outfit with NO GLOVES and any joggers. (Use Israel/Classic parachute bag)
2. Lag C1 with netcut. (Bandwith 5kbps, incoming 7500)
3. Login to gta on C2 and start an invite only session.
4. Equip C2 outfit then re-equip your Israel/Classic parachute bag, Wait for the yellow circle.
5. Go to storymode then fully close GTA.
6. Remove your parachute on C1 then unlag console with netcut.
7. Wait for a "Save Successful" message. (Buy ammo / take easy way out if needed)
8. Quit to storymode.
9. Start an invite only session.
10. You should load in with the joggers associated with the pnts you put on C2 outfit
